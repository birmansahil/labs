Thank You for Submitting Your Work

We are thrilled to have received your product submission! Your creativity and dedication are the driving forces behind what makes our store a unique and vibrant marketplace. Every item we receive adds to the rich tapestry of talent and innovation that defines Humber Merch.

Our team will now begin the review process to ensure your submission meets our quality and eligibility criteria. This thorough review process is essential to maintaining the high standards we set for our store. Typically, this process takes a few days, and we appreciate your patience as we carefully evaluate each aspect of your product.

You will receive an email notification once our review is complete. If we require any additional information or adjustments, we will reach out to you directly. Please keep an eye on your inbox for any communications from us. We aim to make this process as smooth and transparent as possible.

Thank you once again for sharing your creativity with us. We look forward to potentially featuring your product in our store and showcasing the incredible talent within our student community. Your contribution helps build a vibrant, supportive environment where student creativity can thrive.
